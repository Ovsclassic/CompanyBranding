The University of Louisiana at Lafayette has established a fellowships program for the recruitment of superior Master’s students. These fellowships are competitively-awarded.

To be eligible for consideration, applicants must be an entering master’s student with an undergraduate overall GPA of at least 3.5 or a continuing master’s student who has completed no more than 12 hours with an overall graduate GPA of at least 3.3
